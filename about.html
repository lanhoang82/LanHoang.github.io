About me

In January 2022, I quit my job with the United Nations in Geneva, Switzerland to pursue a career in data analytics. I was and still am enticed by the discipline that enables organizations to maximize the value of their data assets for impactful business decisions.

Fastforward to 2023, I now possess ~1 year of experience in business analytics in the financial services sector, as well as ~5 years of global working experience in the public/nonprofit sector (focusing on business and human rights, social and migration policies, as well as the strategic planning and implementation of organizational reform and related change management).

As an avid self-learner, I constantly look to expand my technical and soft skillsets. My current technical competencies include:

  • Quantitative analysis (Excel, R)
  • Predictive analytics (supervised machine learning algorithms in Python and R)
  • Data manipulation (PostgreSQL, Alteryx Designer Core Certified, pandas)
  • Data visualization (Tableau Data Analyst Certified, Power BI, matplotlib)

    • Originally from Vietnam, I am trilingual in Vietnamese and English (native), French (certified advanced level DALF C1).

    When not nerding over data sets, I can be found on a hiking trail somewhere in Switzerland or on the dance floor swiveling to the tunes of 1920s-40s swing jazz.
